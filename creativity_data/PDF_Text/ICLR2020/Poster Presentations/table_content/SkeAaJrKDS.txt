Table E.1: Results on Atari. Scores are final performance averaged over 3 seeds. “Baseline” is thestandard version of R2D2 (Kapturowski et al., 2018). “Controlled” is our version that is controlledto have the same replay ratio as SAVE. The last two rows show median and mean scores, respectively.
