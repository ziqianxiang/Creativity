Deeper capsules are built from a consensus-based aggregationof predictions made for them by shallower capsules. By having capsules activated only when pre-dictions for them are in agreement, this procedure allows for a checking of whether the shallowercapsules share a common viewpoint and can form the corresponding object. This process is termedrouting.
